Arc Testnet Analytics is a lightweight, client-side dashboard that reads recent blocks directly from the Arc Testnet RPC. No backend, no indexing, just quick insights for builders. Shows recent transaction count, unique active addresses, average gas price, average block time, and the most-called contracts in a sampled block window.
